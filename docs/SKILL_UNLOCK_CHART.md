## Damage Scaling Examples

### Destruction Spell Growth (Based on Skill Level)
```
Flames (Fire Bolt path):
- Level 20: 15 + (20 × 0.3) = 21 damage
- Level 50: 15 + (50 × 0.3) = 30 damage
- Level 100: 15 + (100 × 0.3) = 45 damage

Lightning Bolt (Shock path):
- Level 50: 35 + (50 × 0.5) = 60 damage
- Level 75: 35 + (75 × 0.5) = 72.5 damage
- Level 100: 35 + (100 × 0.5) = 85 damage

Meteor Storm (Ultimate):
- Level 90: 50 + (90 × 0.6) = 104 damage
- Level 100: 50 + (100 × 0.6) = 110 damage
```

### Sneak Attack Multipliers (with Backstab Perk)
```
Base Damage: 10 (dagger)

Without Perks:
- One-handed sneak: 10 × 1 = 10 damage
- Bow sneak: 10 × 1 = 10 damage

With Backstab Perk (3 ranks):
- Rank 1: 10 × 3 = 30 damage
- Rank 2: 10 × 4 = 40 damage
- Rank 3: 10 × 5 = 50 damage

With Assassin's Blade (Dagger only):
- 10 × 15 = 150 damage [ULTIMATE]
```

## Key Progression Notes

1. **Early Game (Level 1-30)**: Access to novice spells, basic combat abilities, starter perks
2. **Mid Game (Level 30-60)**: Intermediate spells, advanced combat abilities, prerequisite chain unlocks
3. **Late Game (Level 60-100)**: Ultimate spells, elite perks, full perk tree optimization
4. **Endgame (Level 100 + Full Perk Trees)**: Maximum damage output, all mechanics fully synergized

All content scales smoothly with character progression, ensuring gameplay stays challenging and rewarding throughout.
